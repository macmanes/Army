\section{Expertise related to proposed research}
\cvitem{ }{The primary goal of the MacManes lab is to understand the genomic underpinnings of complex phenotypes and adaptation. This work integrates the power of novel sequencing technologies with ecology, physiology, and behavior. The lab has recognized authority in genome and transcriptome assembly as a developer of the Trinity and Trinotate software packages, and co-author of the recent Assemblathon 2 paper. In addition, the lab has expertise in the quality control and pre-processing of high-throughput sequencing data, with both published manuscripts and software packages. }
